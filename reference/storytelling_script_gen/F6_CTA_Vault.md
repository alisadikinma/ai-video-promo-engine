## 0. Pre-CTA Peak Protocol (v4.0 — MANDATORY)

> **Source:** F7 / Kahneman Peak-End Rule. The CTA must be PRECEDED by a designed emotional Peak. Without Peak → CTA momentum collapses.

**Rule:** Maximum **5 seconds** between Peak and CTA. No new information between Peak and CTA.

| Peak Technique | Execution | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Transformation Reveal** | Before/after payoff lands — hero sees the change | Technical Buyer, Operator |
| **Surprise Data Point** | One stat that reframes everything | Investor, ESG Buyer, Fleet Manager |
| **Music Crescendo + Visual Climax** | Sonic + visual sync at emotional apex | Brand Manifesto, Status Buyer |
| **"Aha" Insight** | The reframe that changes how hero sees the problem | C-Level, Technical |
| **Testimonial Climax** | Real person's emotional turning point | All personas — highest proof value |

**Pre-CTA Bridge Sentence Formula:**
> *"[Identity they're becoming] starts with [one specific next action]."*
> Example: *"Leading with confidence starts with one conversation."*

## 1. CTA 1: C-Level / Direksi — The ROI & Strategic Vision CTA

### Psychological Driver
Legacy threat + stagnation anxiety. The "Success" imagery: Position and Power. Cast a vision where "the threat of failure is over."

### CTA Type: Direct ("Marry Me")
C-Level has authority to commit. Give them a decisive next step.

### Strategy
- Emphasize "Business Transformation" — not product features
- Offer an **Executive ROI Briefing** or **Strategic Audit**
- Frame as competitive dominance, not cost savings

### VO Script Library

| Category | Framework | VO Script |
|---|---|---|
| **Legacy Vision** | Strategic Growth + Direct CTA | *"Don't let your organization's growth be stunted by a confusing narrative. **Schedule your Executive ROI Briefing today.** Let's map out a plan where the threat of market stagnation is over, and your strategic vision is finally clear."* |
| **Competitive Edge** | Position + Power | *"The leaders who act first don't just survive — they define the category. **Book a 30-minute Strategic Vision Session** — we'll show you exactly what the next 18 months look like when you lead instead of follow."* |

### Forbidden: Specs, task lists, implementation details.

## 2. CTA 2: International Buyers & Investors — The Authority & Compliance CTA

### Psychological Driver
Loss Aversion (Kahneman) — 2–3× more motivated to avoid loss than achieve gain.

### CTA Type: Transitional ("Let's Date")
Hero must feel *safe* before committing capital.

### Strategy
- Lead with verified social proof — not promises
- Anchor in compliance language: *certified, risk-free, verified, audited*
- Offer **Risk Assessment Session** or **Compliance Audit**

### VO Script Library

| Category | Framework | VO Script |
|---|---|---|
| **Risk Mitigation** | Loss Aversion + Social Proof | *"Every unvetted AI deployment is a liability waiting to surface. Join the 200+ global enterprises that have already eliminated that risk. **Request your Compliance & Risk Audit today.**"* |
| **Authority** | Certified Trust + Transitional | *"You shouldn't have to take our word for it. Our framework is ISO-certified, independently audited, and battle-tested across three continents. **Download our Global Compliance Dossier.**"* |
| **Partnership** | Shared Journey + Low-Risk Entry | *"The gap between a promising technology and a proven investment is one validated deployment. **Schedule a no-commitment Partnership Discovery Call.**"* |

## 3. CTA 3: Technical Director / Engineer — The Proof & Logic CTA

### Psychological Driver
Fear of implementation failure. Allergic to "marketing speak." Requires peer-to-peer evidence.

### CTA Type: Transitional ("Let's Date")
Build reciprocity before asking for commitment. Evidence first.

### Strategy
- Ground CTA in logic — use Transitional CTAs (whitepaper, sandbox, PoC)
- Reference techniques like **RAG** to prove AI is grounded in trusted data
- Peer-to-peer language, not sales language

### VO Script Library

| **Evidence First** | Logic + Transitional | *"Moving from classification to generative creation requires more than a leap of faith—it requires evidence. **Download our Technical Implementation Roadmap** or request a **Proof of Concept demo.**"* |

### Forbidden: Marketing fluff, "magic" solutions, abstract promises.

## 4. CTA 4: End-Users & Operators — The Ease & Uptime CTA

### Psychological Driver
Feeling "confused and ill-equipped." Responds to **immediate, tangible relief** — not ROI language.

### CTA Type: Direct ("Marry Me") with Low Cognitive Friction
Short. Concrete. Zero jargon. The offer = **relief**, not a decision.

### Strategy
- Skip financial language entirely
- Speak to the **next shift**, not the next quarter
- Offer **free trial, live walkthrough, or hands-on demo**
- Tone: warm, peer-to-peer — Guide as colleague

### VO Script Library

| Category | Framework | VO Script |
|---|---|---|
| **Daily Relief** | Ease + Immediate Uptime | *"You shouldn't spend your shift fighting the tools meant to help you. **Start your free 14-day trial today** — no setup fees, no IT tickets. Just a system that works the first time you touch it."* |
| **Competence** | "Feel Smart Again" + Direct | *"When the system makes sense, you make better decisions. **Watch a 3-minute live walkthrough** and see exactly how your daily workflow changes."* |
| **Fatigue** | Empathy + Frictionless | *"We know you didn't sign up to troubleshoot software every morning. **Book a hands-on demo with one of our operators** — not a salesperson."* |

### Forbidden: ROI percentages, corporate jargon, multi-step CTAs.

## 10. Awareness-Level CTA Routing (v4.0)

> **Source:** F8. CTA intensity must match audience readiness.

| Awareness Level | CTA Type | CTA Intensity | Offer Format | Forbidden |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Unaware** | None / Soft Follow | Zero pressure | Follow social / subscribe / bookmark | Any product mention or commitment ask |
| **Problem-Aware** | Transitional (ultra-soft) | Educational | Free guide / checklist / diagnostic | Sales language of any kind |
| **Solution-Aware** | Transitional | Evidence-based | Whitepaper / demo / comparison tool | Direct purchase or booking |
| **Product-Aware** | Transitional → Direct | Proof-driven | PoC / free trial / consultation | Hype without evidence |
| **Most Aware** | Direct ("Marry Me") | Confident + Urgent | Buy / book / reserve / schedule | Extended storytelling — just close |

**Godfather Offer Rule (v4.0):** For Product-Aware and Most-Aware audiences, the offer should be **too compelling to refuse**. Include a bold guarantee that eliminates all perceived risk: *"If you don't see [specific result] within [timeframe], [guarantee]."*

## 12. CTA Writing Rules (Universal)

1. **Name the Internal Problem first** — hero must feel *seen* before they act.
2. **One action only** — one CTA per script. Two options = no decision.
3. **Offer reduces risk, not adds pressure** — "see for yourself," not "buy now."
4. **Match vocabulary to persona** — use F1 keyword matrix to verify.
5. **Cast the success state** — CTA ends with Won Day glimpse, not product.
6. **Deploy Pride Layer image in final sentence** — close on who hero becomes.
7. **Never name the vehicle/product in the CTA** — hero is the subject.
8. **Match friction to trust threshold** — Skeptic/ESG = Transitional. Status/Commuter = Direct.
9. **Sustainability = personal advantage** — if environmental benefit appears, translate to time/money/status.
10. **Completable in one step** — if hero must think about *how* to respond, CTA has failed.
11. **Peak must precede CTA** — no CTA without emotional momentum (v4.0).
12. **Awareness level determines CTA intensity** — consult §10 routing table (v4.0).
